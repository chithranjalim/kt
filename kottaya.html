Places To Visit In kottayam

Hover over the image below to read more about it

Marmala Waterfall

One of the best and highest waterfalls in Kottayam at a height of 200 feet. It might get dangerous around monsoons, best season to visit is during october where the water will be perfect to take a bath. Please don't venture inside the waterfalls.

Kattikkayam Waterfalls

A good place for young people to have a good time. The walk from road is long and the climb down to waterfall is very steep and slippery. So be cautious if u go..
